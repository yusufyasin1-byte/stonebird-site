Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Stonebird Group ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information across our website and applications.

Our websites

This website (stonebirdgroup.com) does not use cookies, does not require user accounts, and does not collect personal information. We may collect anonymous analytics data such as page views, referral sources, and general geographic location through standard server logs. This data is used solely to improve the website experience.

Our applications

Gridlock: Terminal

Gridlock: Terminal is a free iOS arcade game. The game:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not require a user account
  • Uses Apple Game Center for achievements and leaderboards (subject to Apple's privacy policy)
  • Does not contain third-party advertising or tracking SDKs
  • Stores game progress locally on your device

Spend Scout: Expense Tracker

Spend Scout is designed as a fully private expense tracker. The app:

  • Does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data externally
  • Does not link to bank accounts or financial institutions
  • Does not require account creation or login
  • Stores all spending data locally on your device only
  • Uses Apple Pay Capture only through a user-configured Shortcuts automation; Spend Scout does not directly read Wallet history
  • Does not contain analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs
  • Does not share data with any third party

Your financial data never leaves your device. If you delete the app, your data is removed with it.

Third-party services

Our apps may interact with Apple's built-in services (Game Center, App Store). These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our website and apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at hello@stonebirdgroup.com.
