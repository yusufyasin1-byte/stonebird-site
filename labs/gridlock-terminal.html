The game

Simple to learn. Difficult to master.

Gridlock: Terminal puts you in the control tower. Aircraft stream in from every direction, each heading for a specific helipad or runway. Your job is to draw their flight paths, guide them safely to the ground, and keep the skies from turning into chaos. The longer you survive, the more crowded the terminal becomes.

Features

Everything you need in the tower.

Use a single finger to draw flight paths. Guide helicopters, heavy planes, and jets to their matching landing zones with precision and speed.

How to play

Three steps. Infinite skies.

01

Tap an aircraft

Select any incoming aircraft to begin routing it. Each vehicle type has a matching landing zone — helicopters need helipads, jets need runways.

02

Draw its path

Use your finger to draw a flight path from the aircraft to the correct destination. Avoid other aircraft, turbulence zones, and screen edges.

03

Land and survive

Guide the aircraft to a safe landing. Each successful landing earns points. Each crash brings you closer to game over. Keep the terminal alive.

Watch out

The skies fight back.

🐦

Birds

Flocks cross your flight paths unpredictably, forcing quick reroutes.

🌪️

Turbulence

Turbulent zones disrupt your drawn paths and push aircraft off course.

🛩️

Screen boundaries

Aircraft that fly off the edge are lost. Keep every route inside the terminal zone.

💥

Mid-air collisions

Crossed paths mean disaster. Time your routes to keep aircraft safely apart.

FAQ

Common questions.

What is Gridlock: Terminal?

Gridlock: Terminal is a fast-paced air traffic control arcade game for iOS. You draw flight paths to guide helicopters, planes, and jets to their correct landing zones while avoiding crashes and obstacles.

Is it free to play?

Yes. Gridlock: Terminal is free to download and play on the App Store. Sessions are designed for quick, addictive retries.

What is Daily Dispatch?

Daily Dispatch is a single daily challenge. You get one attempt, one score, and one chance to climb the leaderboard. It resets every 24 hours.

What aircraft types are in the game?

The game features helicopters, heavy planes, and jets. Each type must land at its matching zone — helicopters at helipads, jets at runways.

Does it support Game Center?

Yes. Gridlock: Terminal supports Game Center achievements and global leaderboards so you can compete with players worldwide.
