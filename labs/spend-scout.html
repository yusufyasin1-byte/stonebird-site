Apple Pay Capture

Capture Apple Pay spending with a prebuilt Shortcuts automation.

Spend Scout helps you log Wallet transactions through an on-device Shortcuts automation. It passes only the transaction details you choose, such as amount, merchant, currency, and date.

FAQ

Common questions.

What is Spend Scout?

Spend Scout is a private expense tracker and budget app for iOS that tells you where your month is heading before it's over. As you log expenses, it forecasts your month-end spending so you can adjust before you overspend.

What makes Spend Scout different from other expense trackers?

Most expense trackers and budget apps are backward-looking — they show you what you already spent. Spend Scout is a forward-looking spending tracker. It tells you where your month is heading so you can change course before overspending happens.

How does the spending forecast work?

Spend Scout calculates your daily spending pace against the number of days elapsed in the month, then projects your total spend by month-end. It updates every time you log an expense. Think of it as a budget tracker that looks forward instead of backward.

Do I need to link my bank account?

No. Spend Scout is a fully private expense tracker with no bank linking, no account creation, and no cloud storage. Your spending data lives only on your device. This makes it ideal for people who want to track spending without sharing financial data.

Can Spend Scout track Apple Pay transactions?

Yes, through a prebuilt Shortcuts automation that runs on your device. Spend Scout does not directly read Wallet history or connect to your bank.

Does it work with multiple currencies?

Yes. You can log expenses in different currencies and Spend Scout keeps them organized. A great finance app for people who travel or spend across borders.
