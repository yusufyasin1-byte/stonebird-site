FAQ

Common questions.

What makes Spend Scout different?

Most expense trackers are backward-looking — they show you what you already spent. Spend Scout is forward-looking. It tells you where your month is heading so you can change course before overspending happens.

How does the forecast work?

Spend Scout calculates your daily spending pace against the number of days elapsed in the month, then projects your total spend by month-end. It updates every time you log an expense.

Is my data private?

Completely. There is no bank linking, no account creation, and no cloud storage. Your spending data lives only on your device.

Does it work with multiple currencies?

Yes. You can log expenses in different currencies and Spend Scout keeps them organized. Perfect for people who travel or spend across borders.
