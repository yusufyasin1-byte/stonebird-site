Apple Pay expense tracking with Shortcuts.

FAQ

Apple Pay tracking questions.

Can Spend Scout track Apple Pay transactions?

Yes, through a prebuilt Shortcuts automation that runs on your device. Spend Scout does not directly read Wallet history or connect to your bank.

Does Spend Scout scrape Wallet or Apple Pay history?

No. Spend Scout does not scrape Wallet, read Wallet history directly, or use a third-party transaction aggregator. The Shortcuts automation passes only the transaction details you choose.
