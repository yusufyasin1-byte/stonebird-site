FAQ

No-bank-login questions.

Can I use Spend Scout without linking a bank account?

Yes. Spend Scout is built for no-bank-login expense tracking. You log spending yourself and can use supported on-device helpers such as receipt Smart Fill and Apple Pay Shortcuts.

Does Spend Scout use a transaction aggregator?

No. Spend Scout does not use a third-party transaction aggregator and does not connect directly to your bank or Wallet history.
