FAQ

Cash spending questions.

Can Spend Scout track cash spending?

Yes. Spend Scout is designed for manual cash expense tracking alongside cards, transfers, receipts, and Apple Pay entries captured through Shortcuts.

Can I track cash without a bank account?

Yes. Spend Scout does not require bank linking or account creation to track cash spending.
