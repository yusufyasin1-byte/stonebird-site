Stonebird Labs

Products presented as long-term assets, not experiments without a point.

Stonebird Labs is the product studio inside Stonebird Group. It builds focused Apple-platform software and small digital products with clear ownership, privacy-conscious defaults, and product pages that explain what each product does before asking for attention.

Stonebird Labs facts and product directory
What is Breadcrumb?

Breadcrumb is a native macOS clipboard history app for finding copied text, images, and nearby copied items with source app context. It is pre-launch and positioned around local-first recovery rather than cloud clipboard sync.

What is Gridlock: Terminal?

Gridlock: Terminal is a free iPhone air traffic control arcade game. Players draw flight paths, land aircraft, play Daily Dispatch, and compete through Game Center without ads or in-app purchases.

What is Spend Scout?

Spend Scout is a private iPhone expense tracker that forecasts where monthly spending is heading. It is pre-launch and designed for manual logging, Apple Pay Shortcuts capture, receipts, currencies, and no bank login.
