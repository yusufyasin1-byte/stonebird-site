Operating principles

Patient ownership. Clear execution. No unnecessary noise.

01

Long-term ownership

We prefer patient control over short-term exits and build for assets that can endure.

02

Disciplined capital allocation

Capital is allocated only where the downside is understood and the path to durable value is credible.

03

Simple, durable businesses

We favor products, services, and assets that remain useful across cycles.

04

Design-led execution

Product, brand, and operating detail are treated as strategic leverage, not decoration.

05

Compounding over speculation

We avoid fashionable noise and focus on decisions that improve with time.
